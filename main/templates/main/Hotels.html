Hotel Naveen Lakeside

Spreading across 9 acres of lush greenery, Hotel Naveen Lakeside is conveniently situated within 1 km from Unkal Lake. For the price, this hotel is phenomenal. The pool was large and clean, the exercise room was very well equipped, the spa is quite good, the breakfast that’s included with the price was very good. The staff was friendly and helpful. It’s only 15 minutes from the airport which is quite convenient but above all else , the hotel sits on the end of a beautiful lake and it’s very quiet for being so close to the busy city.
